title = "Scenes imprinted"
date = 2026-02-10

Soft is the glow against the room.
Illuminating illustrations imprinted   
against the mind.   +++

Cesspool of shades offer little comfort when unknown; Scenes unfamiliar are dangerous.
But when they are imprinted the mind fills in the gaps, stabilizing the image.
The image is aged, thick with history, fraught with silence. 

Stale are the memories ripped from the room.   
Fermenting in the corner of conciousness,   
but not lost.
